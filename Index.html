Student Profile: Elijah Izeoma

Biography | Reasons for Joining AltSchool Africa | Goals

Biography

Elijah Izeoma with Altschool ID ALT/SOE/024/0367. Born 2nd October and hails from Rivers State, has a passion for technology and design. Growing up being fascinated about video games and mobile phones, he evolved into a tech-savvy person, Elijah later developed interest in computers and software development. He enjoys learning and experimenting with new knowledge pertaining to web technologies. Elijah is eager to translate his hobby into a professional career.

Reasons for Joining Altschool Africa

Elijah chose to join Altschool Africa because of their excellent reputation in the field of software engineering. Altschool Africa offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers both the theoretical and practical aspects of web development. Also, there is provision for opportunities for hands-on experience through assignments and projects which Elijah finds very appealing.

Goals

Elijah's primary goal is to become a proficient front-end engineer. He aims to master HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and to become well-versed in modern frameworks. Elijah also hopes to build a strong portfolio of projects that demonstrate his skills and creativity. In the long term, he aspires to work for a leading tech company where he can contribute to innovative web solutions.
